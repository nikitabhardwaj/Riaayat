ABOUT US

RIAAYAT is an online donation platform which aims to connect credible non-governmental organizations across India with underprivileged or needful people.

RIAAYAT uses technology to close the information gap between the needful and the helper by providing socially-conscious organizations and people with relevant information at the right time.

“One way to help the society is to lend a hand to those already helping the society"

RIAAYAT intends to do exactly that. Help the helper.
